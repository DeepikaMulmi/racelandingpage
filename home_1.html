1. How can I download from Play store?

First, open Google playstore and sign-in with your Google Account username and password.

2. Search our 'RACE Groups' application

After loging into your playstore account,click into apps section and go to search button and type 'RACE Groups'.

3.Install 'RACE Groups' application.

After searching 'RACE Groups', click install button beside application name and wait for 100% installation process.

3. Open 'RACE Groups' application.

After installation process is completed, open 'RACE Groups' application and create new account for users.

1. How to register new user in your application?

Click 'Sign Up' and register page will open. Then, fill up the required details such as Name, Email and password.

2. How can I install your application?

Open Google Playstore and search 'RACE Groups' and install it & wait for it to install. After installation process is completed, login or sign up if you are new user.

3. Is your application paid?
